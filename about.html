About The App

This app created for the purpose of tracking the reported cases of the novel coronavirus 2019-nCoV (or COVID-19) in China and other countries around the world, with push notification - if enabled. It includes interactive dashboards -New ones added continually- for the virus stats. The data provided here are collected and aggregated from several sources manually, as it needs to be confirmed first, while some pulled automatically, so your support is deeply appreciated. Reach out to me for any suggestions or improvments on tamim.io.dev at gmail.com
